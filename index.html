About The Wild Ones

The Wild Ones is led by James Moak, a proud Louisville, Kentucky native. With a background as an engineer at Amazon, Fitbit, Google, and a graduate of Georgia Tech, James brings a unique blend of expertise to the table.

After gaining invaluable experience in Silicon Valley, James returned home, combining that cutting-edge Silicon Valley knowledge with Ohio Valley grit. This unique perspective allows The Wild Ones to provide cheat codes to your Ohio Valley region business, ranging from educational discussions to integration-ready technical implementations.
